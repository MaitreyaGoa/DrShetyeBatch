⏱ Inside the Test
  • Timer counts down at the top — submit before it hits zero
  • Tap any option (A / B / C / D) to select your answer
  • You can change your answer any time before submitting
  • Tap "Submit Test" when done — never just close the tab
  • After submit: see your score, rank, and correct answers with explanations

Test Types Explained
Know what each category is for
📅 Daily Quiz Go ↗

15 mixed questions every day. Do this first every morning. 15 minutes. Keeps your brain active on all 3 subjects daily.

📆 Monthly Full Test Go ↗

100 questions, 90 minutes, all 4 subjects. Do at end of each month to track overall progress. Your rank appears on the merit list.

🏛 GSSC Mock Tests Go ↗

Simulates real GSSC exam — 50Q, 60 min. For Police Constable, Clerk, Junior posts. Attempt after you're comfortable with basics.

🎯 GPSC / JSO Go ↗

60 questions, heavy on Reasoning & Logic. For Group A & B / JSO aspirants only.

📄 PYQ Papers Go ↗

Real questions from past GSSC exams. Highest priority — do all PYQs at least twice. This is the closest to the actual exam.

📝 Part Tests Go ↗

Subject-wise focused tests, 10–30Q each. Use when you want to improve one specific subject quickly.

🔬 Topic Drills Go ↗

10–15 questions on a single topic — Profit & Loss, Tenses, Blood Relations etc. Best for targeting your weak areas.

Tips & Help
Common issues + pro tips
🔇
No distractions during tests. Silent phone, close WhatsApp. Attempt in one sitting — just like the real exam.
📖
Always read explanations. After submitting, read every explanation — even for correct answers. That's where real learning happens.
⏱
Watch the timer. Time management is key in GSSC. Practice finishing 10 seconds before time ends.
🏅
Check the merit list. After each test tap "🏅 Toppers" — compare your score with the topper. That gap is your target.
📵
Don't close the tab during a test. Always tap Submit first — closing the browser mid-test may not save your answers.
🆘 Common Problems
  • Wrong password? Check the password list above or the WhatsApp channel
  • Test not starting? Check it has the green "Live" badge — "Soon" tests are locked
  • Score not saved? Always tap Submit before closing the page
  • Page not loading? Check internet, then refresh
